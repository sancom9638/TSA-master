About Us

Space Horizon is a revolutionary space tourism company founded in 2022 with a mission to make space accessible to everyone. Our team of expert astronauts and engineers have been at the forefront of space exploration and technology for over a decade. With our state-of-the-art spacecrafts and cutting-edge launch systems, we're able to offer unparalleled experiences for thrill-seekers and space enthusiasts alike.

Our Fleet

Our fleet of specially designed space balloons offers a unique and unparalleled experience for thrill-seekers and space enthusiasts alike. Soar to new heights and marvel at the breathtaking views of the cosmos from the comfort of our state-of-the-art balloons. With a capacity to carry up to 12 passengers at a time, you and your friends can embark on the adventure of a lifetime.

Future Development

Space Horizon is proud to announce a significant breakthrough in our research and development department. Our dedicated scientists and engineers have been working tirelessly to revolutionize the space tourism industry, and we are thrilled to announce that their efforts have finally paid off. With this new technology, we can offer our customers even more exciting and immersive space travel experiences.

Benefit Of Space Horizon

At Space Horizon, we understand the importance of protecting our planet for future generations. That 's why we have made it a priority to ensure that our space tourism operations have a minimal impact on the environment. We have implemented a series of measures to reduce our carbon footprint, from using sustainable materials in the construction of our spacecrafts to powering our facilities with renewable energy sources.
